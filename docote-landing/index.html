FAQ

Early questions we expect

Is DoCoTe replacing Confluence or internal docs?

No. The first goal is to generate better documentation inputs and release summaries from delivery work. Teams can then publish or refine them in their existing tools.

Does it require deep repository access from day one?

No. The first version can work with Jira issue data plus linked PR or implementation context, keeping the MVP focused and easier to adopt.

Who is this for first?

Teams with structured delivery processes, Jira usage, and recurring pain around documentation, handoff, and release communication.
